Security & Compliance

At MediSync, we understand that data security is paramount in healthcare. Our platform is designed with multiple layers of protection to keep sensitive patient information safe.

  • HIPAA compliant infrastructure with end-to-end encryption
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access controls for granular permission management
  • Automatic backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Detailed audit logs for tracking all data access and modifications
